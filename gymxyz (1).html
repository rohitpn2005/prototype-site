Our Story

More Than A Gym. A Transformation Lab.

Founded in 2025 in the heart of Kasavanahalli, Gym XYZ was built on a simple belief, that world-class fitness should be accessible to everyone, from first-time students to seasoned athletes.

We've built a space that's welcoming, inclusive and engineered purely for results, modern imported equipment, certified coaches and personalised programming, backed by a community that pushes you to become your strongest self.
